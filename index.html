Our Mission

Driven by excellence and a passion for fundamental science, our mission is to improve the way the fields of AI, data science, and protein engineering are connected. We are developing a unique AI-plateform based on our groundbreaking research on protein sequence variation and functionality. Our technology leverages recent advances in protein-specific large language models and graph neural networks to conduct drug discovery based solely on the protein sequence of an identified target. Our aim is to simplify and accelerate early-stage discovery, reducing failure rates, resource use, and energy consumption.
